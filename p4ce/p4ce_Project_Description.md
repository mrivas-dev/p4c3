# Project Description — Hybrid Strength for CrossFit Athletes

## Overview

A mobile-first iOS training application designed specifically for CrossFit and hybrid athletes who want structured strength progression without sacrificing conditioning performance.

The app combines adaptive strength programming, fatigue-aware training recommendations, and ultra-fast workout logging into a streamlined offline-first experience optimized for real gym usage.

Unlike traditional fitness apps that focus only on bodybuilding or generic workout tracking, this platform is built around the realities of hybrid training:

* accumulated fatigue
* Olympic lifting complexity
* fluctuating readiness
* concurrent conditioning volume
* progressive overload management

The system intelligently generates personalized strength cycles centered around key compound and Olympic movements:

* Snatch
* Clean & Jerk
* Back Squat
* Front Squat
* Deadlift
* Bench Press

Programs adapt based on athlete readiness, training consistency, RPE feedback, and progression history.

The experience is intentionally minimal and fast:
athletes should be able to open the app, see today’s training, log performance between sets in seconds, and continue training without distractions.

# Vision

Create the simplest and most effective strength companion for CrossFit and hybrid athletes.

The goal is to bridge the gap between:

* spreadsheet-based programming
* generic workout trackers
* elite performance systems

while maintaining a lightweight, athlete-focused experience.

The app should feel like:

> “a training notebook designed by people who actually lift.”

---

# Core Problem

CrossFit and hybrid athletes often struggle with:

* balancing strength progression with WOD fatigue
* managing recovery and accumulated stress
* tracking progression consistently
* adapting training intensity dynamically
* using spreadsheets or overly complex software during training

Most current solutions are either:

* too generic
* too bodybuilding-oriented
* too data-heavy
* too slow to use during workouts

This app solves that by providing:

* adaptive strength progression
* fatigue-aware recommendations
* ultra-fast workout logging
* intelligent programming automation
* clean performance analytics

# Target Audience

## Primary Users

Intermediate to advanced:

* CrossFit athletes
* hybrid athletes
* functional fitness competitors
* strength-focused CrossFit practitioners

## User Characteristics

* train 4–6 times per week
* perform both conditioning and strength work
* care about long-term progression
* value speed and simplicity
* often train while fatigued
* dislike spreadsheet management

# Core Features

## Adaptive Strength Programming

* Auto-generated 4–5 day cycles
* Periodized progression models
* Deload and fatigue management
* Percentage-based loading
* Dynamic progression adjustments

---

## Readiness & Fatigue Tracking

Daily readiness scoring using:

* perceived fatigue
* soreness
* sleep quality
* session performance
* RPE trends

Training intensity adapts automatically based on readiness state.

---

## Workout Logging

Fast and frictionless training capture:

* sets
* reps
* weight
* RPE
* notes
* completion status

Designed for one-handed use between sets.

---

## Olympic Lift Support

Specialized progression for:

* Snatch
* Clean & Jerk

Including:

* volume management
* technical exposure balancing
* fatigue-sensitive programming

---

## Analytics & Progress Tracking

Performance visualization:

* estimated 1RM trends
* volume accumulation
* consistency tracking
* fatigue correlation
* progression velocity
* PR detection

## Offline-First Experience

The app functions entirely offline using local device storage.

Benefits:

* instant launch
* zero loading states
* no authentication friction
* reliable gym usage
* private athlete data
